NORTHEAST BUCKET GETTERS

Northeast Bucket Getters (NBG) is a youth basketball development program serving boys and girls from 12U to 17U. We are committed to helping young athletes grow through elite basketball training, competitive opportunities, and positive mentorship. Our mission is to foster a love for the game while building character, discipline, and life skills that extend far beyond the court.

We believe in developing the whole athlete‚Äîmentally, physically, and emotionally. Whether a player is just starting their journey or preparing for high school or collegiate competition, our program meets them where they are and pushes them to achieve their full potential.
